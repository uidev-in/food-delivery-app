Key Features

Our expertise extends to providing top-notch features to quickly launch your
own food delivery solution

Route Planning

Never be late again to deliver an order. With ExMyB's powerful route planning feature the admin can easily optimise and plan routes for drivers and track their progress in real-time. Now plan your routes more efficiently and save your delivery time.

Geofencing

The admin can easily create multiple geofences and mark the areas of the drivers to operate in different geofences. Each team can be assigned a geofence and can easily fulfil the supply in those areas. The admin can align more drivers in areas where there is more demand.

Payment Methods

Nowadays every platform needs to be implemented with powerful payment gateways like Razorpay, paytm, etc to smoothen the payment process and access to payment summary for your vendors. The payment gateways also ensure the safe and secured method of payment.

Order Management

The admin panel can be customised to meet the demands of the admin and it also empowers the admin and vendors to keep LIVE track of the delivery agent and each of their orders. The vendor can now accept/reject the order from the vendor portal.

Notifications

Vendors and Customers can be kept informed of promotional offers, delivery and order status with the regular updates in real-time through the notification feature, vendors and delivery agents will get LIVE alerts on their devices.

Feedback and ratings

Get real-time feedback and rating of your order and delivery agent from the customers. Now the vendors or delivery agents can also rate the customers and their behaviour. The vendors can ensure to improve the quality through the feedback.

Analytics

The Analytics tool has become important for Vendors get a transparent view of all sales data and commission structure and delivery agents are always up to date with total earnings and incentives through the analytics available on their app.
